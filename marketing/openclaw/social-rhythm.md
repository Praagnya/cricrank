# Social rhythm — X / Instagram (for OpenClaw or manual calendar)

Suggested cadence for CricRank; tune to your bandwidth.

## X (Twitter)

- **Match days**: 1–2 posts — fixture reminder, then result / community angle (not raw score spam unless that’s your brand).
- **Build in public**: 1–2×/week — small shipped fix, lesson learned, or “why we built X this way”.
- **Threads**: occasional deep dive (settlement logic, fairness, streak math) when you have a clear story.

## Instagram

- **Feed / carousel**: 1×/week — feature highlight, screenshot with short caption, or “how to play in 3 slides”.
- **Stories**: match-day stickers, polls (“who wins?”), repost UGC with permission.
- **Reels**: 15–30s screen recordings of picking a team or seeing leaderboard movement.

## OpenClaw-style recurring tasks (ideas)

Map these to your agent’s cron or checklist; exact CLI depends on your OpenClaw install.

1. **Weekly**: draft 5 X posts + 2 IG captions from `brand-context.md` + recent changelog/commits (if you allow repo read).
2. **Match week**: draft day-of posts for listed fixtures from your public schedule or API.
3. **Engagement**: surface mentions/replies that need a human answer (agent summarizes; you reply).

## Approval

Until automated posting is proven safe: **output drafts to a file or chat → you paste or schedule** in native apps / Buffer / etc.
